# sales-trend-Analyzer
sales trend analyzer
📊 Data-Driven Storytelling: Built an interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing Sales, Profit, Discounts, and Orders across multiple dimensions.

🌍 Geographic Insights: Visualized Sales by City across major Indian markets (Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Kolkata, etc.), enabling regional performance comparison.

🎯 Promotion Effectiveness: Compared discount strategies — Clearance Sale and New Year Special drove 70% average discounts, while Festive Diwali had minimal impact (10%).

📈 Trend Analysis: Designed a Sales Trend by Period (2020–2024) chart, highlighting seasonal spikes and long-term growth patterns.

💡 Profitability Correlation: Created a Profit vs Net Sales scatter plot, revealing a strong linear relationship between revenue and profitability.

🏆 Product Performance: Conducted Top/Bottom 5 Analysis — Apple iPhone 14, MacBook Air, and Sony Bravia led in sales and profit, while FMCG items like Colgate Toothpaste and Dove Soap showed minimal contribution.

📦 Quantity Insights: Balanced view of high-selling electronics vs lower-moving consumer goods, helping identify inventory optimization opportunities.

⚙️ Technical Skills: Leveraged Fact Tables, Dim Tables, and Measures to build robust data models, ensuring accurate filtering and drill-down analysis.

🚀 Business Impact: Delivered actionable insights for sales strategy, discount planning, and product portfolio optimization.
